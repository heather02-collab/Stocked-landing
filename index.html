The problem

Someone always ends up owning pantry.

It's rarely their main job. It's reactive, scattered across tools, and just important enough to keep pulling their attention. The people who can afford to outsource it do. Everyone else is left piecing it together as they go.

  • 📋
    Orders tracked across inboxes, spreadsheets, and memory — none of which talk to each other
  • 📦

  • 🔍

How it works

Set it up once.
Stay ahead from there.

01
Connect your vendors
Add the vendors you already use, set your monthly budget, and drop in your regular items. Takes about 10 minutes.
02
Log orders as they happen
Add orders manually or import from CSV. Every order updates your budget and vendor spend automatically.
03
Stay ahead without the scramble
See budget health, recent orders, and what needs attention next — before low stock, overspend, or vendor chaos turns into a problem.
